Diary

Your private, encrypted journal — fully offline.

What it does

  • Daily entries — write text with formatting, set mood, tag, and save.
  • Photos & videos — attach to any entry. Encrypted on device.
  • Voice notes — tap 🎤 to record audio (max 5 min).
  • Calendar view — month grid with mood at a glance.
  • On this day — see entries from same date in past years.
  • Search — fast prefix search across titles and bodies.
  • Tags — organize entries; tap a chip to filter.
  • Backup & restore — encrypted .diarybackup file you control.
  • Dark & light mode — toggle ☀/🌙 anywhere.
  • PWA — install to home screen, runs offline.

How to use

1.
Create an entry

Tap + in bottom nav. Add title, pick a mood, write. Tap Save.

2.
Attach media

+ Photo / Video picks from library. 📷 Camera opens the camera. 🎤 Record starts a voice note (tap again to stop).

3.
Browse

Use List for chronological view, Calendar for month overview. Tap a calendar day to open that entry, or to start a new one for that date.

4.
Search & filter

Type in the search bar. Tap a tag chip on any entry to filter the timeline by that tag. Tap the pink filter pill to clear.

5.
Back up

Settings → Encrypted Backup downloads a .json file. Save it somewhere safe (cloud drive, AirDrop). Restore on another device with the same passcode.

6.
If your device dies

Open Diary on a new device, set up with your passcode, then go to Settings → Restore Backup and pick the file. All entries and media return.

Privacy & security

  • Local-only. No server, no cloud, no analytics. Everything stays in your browser/device.
  • Encrypted. Entries, photos, videos, audio — all AES-GCM 256-bit. Your passcode derives the key.
  • No recovery. If you forget your passcode, data is gone forever. Back up.
  • Auto-lock. App locks after 2 minutes in background.

Tips

  • Long-press the calendar month title to jump back to the current month.
  • Drafts auto-save every 3 seconds while editing — close the tab safely.
  • Theme toggle (☀/🌙) sits next to the version label on the lock screen and in every header.
  • Hard refresh: tap ↻ next to the version on the lock screen to fetch the latest update.
